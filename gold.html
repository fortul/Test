Gold ETF Price Chart (GLD)

This interactive chart allows you to explore the historical closing prices of the SPDR Gold Shares ETF (GLD). Use the controls below to choose your desired frequency—daily, monthly or yearly—and select a time range. The data is loaded from a local JSON file (data/gld_data.json). To keep the information current, run the included update_data.py script regularly to fetch the latest prices.
