About Us

Our Story

Gold ETF Insights was founded by a small group of finance enthusiasts in Lugano, Switzerland. We believe that everyone should have access to clear information about investing in gold through exchange‑traded funds. Inspired by the transparency and accessibility of SPDR Gold Shares—each share represents one‑tenth of an ounce of gold held in secure vaults—we set out to build a resource where investors can educate themselves and track price movements without having to manage physical bullion. Our mission is to demystify gold investing and to empower visitors with tools and knowledge to make informed decisions.

When GLD was launched in 2004, it revolutionized how the average investor could gain exposure to gold. At Gold ETF Insights we continue that tradition by offering a free charting tool, curated articles and guides. Whether you are a seasoned investor or just starting out, we hope our site becomes a valuable companion on your journey to understand the role of gold in a diversified portfolio.
